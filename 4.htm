Betel leaf

The content of betel leaves is phenyl propane, essential oil, estragol, kavibetol, caryophyllene, cadinene, tannin, hydroxycavicol, kavicol, allylpyrokatekol, cyneole, diastase, starch and others. All of these contents play an active role for your health, this is evidenced by one of the usefulness of betel leaf that can cure asthma. Boil water betel leaf can clean your eyes by dripping like eye medicine, but who would have thought it stalks betel leaf can help grow eyelashes. Way by applying the betel leaf stalk with gel aloe vera and then apply on the roots of eyelashes. Or it could be by growing the betel leaf until soft, mix a little gel aloe vera and then apply on the lashes.
